What Does Two Way Traffic Tell Us ?

Image result for what does two way traffic traffic sign tell us The purpose of the TWO-WAY TRAFFIC AHEAD sign is to warn drivers travelling on a one-way street or highway that they are approaching a roadway section where two-way traffic is in operation, and that their ability to pass freely will be restricted by opposing traffic.
